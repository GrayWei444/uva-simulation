================================================================================
v6.7 花青素機制摘要
================================================================================

機制: FW-based (synthesis_rate = FW × ...)

合成公式:
  Step 1: base_per_fw = day × light_rate + night × dark_rate
  Step 2: induced_per_fw = V_max × Stress / (K_stress_anth + Stress)
  Step 3: synthesis_rate = FW × (base_per_fw + induced_per_fw)
  Step 4: dAnth/dt = synthesis_rate - k_deg × Anth

驅動變量:
  - 瞬時 Stress (非 E_stress) ✅
  - 鮮重 FW (非 LAI) ✅

單位: [kg Anth / (kg FW · s)]

================================================================================
常用公式
================================================================================

Stress 損傷率:
  damage = k_damage × I_UVA × vulnerability × intraday_factor ×
           nonlinear_factor × circadian_penalty

Stress 修復率:
  repair = k_repair × Stress × (base_capacity + carbon_bonus)

生長抑制:
  inhibition = Stress / (K_stress + Stress)
  dXd/dt = dXd_base × (1 - β_photo × inhibition)  [if dXd_base > 0]

動態 LDMC:
  DW:FW = base × (1 + sensitivity × Stress / (K_ldmc + Stress))
